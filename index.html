Radiosurgery - treating brain tumors:

The treatment of brain tumors (neuro-oncology) represents an interdisciplinary and technical challenge. The Gamma Knife Center Bochum uses a special form of radiation therapy: radiosurgery. The Gamma Knife Center Bochum specializes in the treatment of brain tumors (neuro-oncology). The treatment requires cooperation between the medical disciplines of neuro-oncology / radiation therapy / neuroradiology / neurosurgery.
